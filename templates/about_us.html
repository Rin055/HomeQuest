About HomeQuest

The Vision Behind HomeQuest

At HomeQuest, we are passionate about creating spaces that resonate with your lifestyle and personal taste. Our vision is simple: to transform your home into a sanctuary that mirrors your individuality.

Personal Introduction

Hi, I’m Mariam Koberidze, the founder of HomeQuest. I started this brand to offer beautiful, high-quality furniture and home decor that can transform any space into a comfortable and stylish retreat. At HomeQuest, I’ve carefully curated pieces that blend timeless elegance with modern design, making it easy to create a home you’ll love.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for interior design and an eye for detail, HomeQuest started as a vision to bring quality furniture to those who seek sophistication and style. Today, we’re proud to offer an exclusive collection that transforms homes into dream spaces.

Customer Approach

Your satisfaction is our top priority. From helping you select the perfect piece to ensuring timely delivery and set-up, we are here for you every step of the way. We are committed to providing a seamless shopping experience, offering personalized recommendations and support to help you create the home of your dreams.

Mission Statement

Our mission with HomeQuest is to help you transform your home with curated furniture and decor that perfectly fits your lifestyle. We source only the finest pieces that combine both beauty and practicality, ensuring your home feels both stylish and welcoming.
